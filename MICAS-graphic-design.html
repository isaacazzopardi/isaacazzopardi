Summer Celebration

Event branding, poster and invitation design Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator & InDesign â€” 2019
The MICAS Summer Celebration was an event held in July 2019 in Valletta, Malta. It consisted of a presentation by curator Edith Devaney (Artists & their Archives) and a video projection of JUMPSTART, a history of Maltese contemporary art. Having access to the JUMPSTART archive, I constructed a timeline design as the centrepiece for the branding, enclosing it with the event title in light orange.



p.s. MICAS hires a design agency for most of its design needs. As an only in-house designer, my role is often to step in for any shortcomings the design agency may have had; missing visuals, or on-the-fly needs. This is the only project I designed in full at MICAS.
