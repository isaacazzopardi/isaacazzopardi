Pepren

branding, typography — Adobe Photoshop, Figma & Apple Notes — 2022
Pepren is a developing personal brand, conceived as a moniker under which to represent my work. The inspiration came from Gavin Strange who presents his work under Jam Factory. The name started out as my gaming moniker—it doesn’t mean anything, but it has an energetic, popping sound to it that I love. Building on previous typography sketches, many different visual ideas for Pepren were developed on iOS Notes and vectorised in Figma. The final gif was a way to showcase different typographic styles within the logo. I have also used this project to learn about developing my own typeface.
