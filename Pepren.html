Pepren

branding, typography — Adobe Photoshop, Figma & Apple Notes — 2022
Challenge:

As someone who finds type challenging, this was a great way to have fun with lettering, and perhaps discover and learn a new approach to typography.
Brief:

To design a personal brand under which to represent myself and my graphic design work.



Pepren is a moniker I have used for gaming. It doesn't really mean anything, but it has a spritely, positive ring to it.

(Well, 'pep' does mean "to have energy and high spirits; liveliness".)









The starting point for this project were letter sketches I had made on a squared notebook, creating ridged leter as the first sketches shown here. To develop these sketches further I used Apple Notes on an iPad Pro, which I find handy for fast sketches and idea generation. After sketching, I screenshot these onto Figma to build as vectors.
I decided to embrace the variety of sketches I made, which as someone with ADHD and a lot of interests suits me well. I used Photosohp to create a gif logo. I hope to make more sketches for this, and also, use this as a stepping stone towards making my first typeface.
